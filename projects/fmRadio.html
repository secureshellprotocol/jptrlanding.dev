FM Radio Receiver

So from what i've found, an FM Radio Receiver consists of a few key components:

  • An FM Tuner
  • Radio Frequency to Intermediate Frequency
    • Radio Frequency is picked up in FM Freq. range
    • Converted to Intermediate Frequency (required for high freq. compatability)
  • IF Filter
  • Demodulator
  • Amplifier
